Household Relocation

Moving to a new home should be exciting, not stressful. At Urban Mitra, we take the burden off your shoulders by managing every aspect of your household relocation. Our team ensures safe packing, careful loading, and efficient transportation of your belongings. We use high-quality packing materials to safeguard fragile items, ensuring they reach your new home intact. Whether you're moving across the city or to a different state, our team handles everything with precision. We also offer unpacking services, helping you settle in quickly. With our real-time tracking system, you’ll always know where your belongings are, making your move seamless and stress-free.

Corporate Office Shifting

Relocating an office is a complex process that requires careful planning and execution. Urban Mitra specializes in corporate office shifting, ensuring minimal downtime and a smooth transition. Our team is trained to handle office furniture, IT equipment, and confidential documents with utmost care. We provide end-to-end solutions, including packing, labeling, transportation, unloading, and reassembling at the new location. Our strategic approach ensures that your business operations resume as quickly as possible. With customized moving plans and flexible scheduling, we cater to businesses of all sizes, making your office relocation efficient and hassle-free.

Intercity Relocation

Moving to a different city comes with its own set of challenges, but with Urban Mitra, it’s easier than ever. We provide reliable and secure long-distance moving services tailored to your needs. Our expert team ensures that your belongings are packed securely to withstand long journeys. We use advanced logistics planning and tracking systems to ensure timely and safe delivery to your new destination. Whether you’re moving for work, family, or any other reason, we take care of the logistics so you can focus on settling into your new city. With transparent pricing and dedicated customer support, we make intercity relocation stress-free and efficient.

Packing & Unpacking

Packing is one of the most critical aspects of a successful move. Our professional packers use industry-standard materials to protect your belongings from damage.
