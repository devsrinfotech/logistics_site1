ABOUT US

Navigating your supply
chain with precision

At Urban Mitra, we believe in making every move smooth and stress-free. Our journey began over a decade ago with a mission to provide reliable, efficient, and affordable logistics solutions. Today, we are a trusted name in the industry, known for our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction. Our vision is to redefine the moving experience through innovation, professionalism, and a customer-first approach.

  • Experience & Expertise – A decade of industry knowledge ensures smooth and secure moving.
  • End-to-End Solutions – We manage everything from packing to unpacking.
  • Affordable & Transparent Pricing – No hidden costs, just honest pricing.
  • Advanced Tracking System – Stay updated with real-time tracking of your belongings.
  • Eco-Friendly Packing Solutions – Sustainable and high-quality packing materials to protect both your items and the environment.
  • 24/7 Customer Support – Assistance whenever you need it for a stress-free move.
